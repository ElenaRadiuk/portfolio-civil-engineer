About

I’m Daniel Stan, a licensed civil engineer who turns complex infrastructure challenges into resilient, cost-effective realities.

Over the past decade I’ve led the design and delivery of structural, transportation, and site-development projects from concept through construction. My work pairs rigorous engineering with disciplined project controls — the result is infrastructure that performs for generations and is delivered on budget and ahead of schedule.

I’ve managed portfolios of 17+ concurrent projects, implemented quality programs that hold defect rates below one percent, and led value-engineering efforts that returned millions in savings to clients and owners.
